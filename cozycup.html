About Us

Café Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate coffee aficionados, COZYCUP Coffee House was born from a simple dream: to create a neighborhood haven where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the warmth of genuine hospitality fill the air. We believe that a great cup of coffee is more than just a beverage; it's an experience. That's why we meticulously source our beans from sustainable farms, roast them to perfection, and train our baristas to craft each drink with precision and care. Our commitment extends to our delectable pastries, baked fresh daily using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. At COZYCUP, we value community, quality, and the simple joy of a perfectly brewed cup.
